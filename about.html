About Me

My name is Yeabsera Tilahun, and I was born on June 3rd, 2003, in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I’m a third-year software engineering student at Addis Ababa Institute of Technology, but I’ve always enjoyed exploring new things. That’s how I got into copywriting.

I’ve always been into football and helping out at my Sunday school, where I learned a lot about teamwork and leadership. Over time, I worked on a few personal businesses, mainly in sales, and realized how important good writing can be for attracting customers.

One day, I came across a YouTube video about copywriting. It was a game-changer. I started learning more, practicing, and improving my skills. Soon, I was writing for businesses, helping them grow with words that connect and persuade.

I also spend time volunteering, tutoring kids, and helping elderly people. This has taught me how to communicate in a way that people understand and appreciate.

Today, I use what I’ve learned to help businesses grow through effective copywriting. It’s not just about writing; it’s about making messages that work.
